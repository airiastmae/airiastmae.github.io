VR-GALLERY OF ARTWORKS MADE IN KURESSAARE REGIONAL TRAINING CENTRE

To practice different possibilities that 3D can offer I got introduced to A-frame framework which became very handy to display the artworks that were made during the studies in Kuressaare Regional Training Centre between 2017-2019 in the classes of painting, drawing, form study and color theory.

VR-gallery of artworks made in Kuressaare RTC.
Move around with arrows/WASD keys, turn view with mouse. It's possible to move through the walls to outdoors.
