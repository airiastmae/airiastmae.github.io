COMMEMORATIVE SCHOOL BADGE WITH QR-CODE

Co-joined classes of composition in visual arts and vector graphics have made it possible to create the commemorative badges for the school. I decided to introduce my studies based on the field of my studies and therefore I made them interactive through QR-code that leads to the according speciality website on Kuressaare Regional Training Centre.
