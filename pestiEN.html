PILLE PESTI WEBSITE PROTOTYPE

​ Website prototype for Pille Pesti was created during traineeship.

Pille's website "Amrayoga" needed refreshing both in content and design. Pille voted for tender, delicate tones but also concreteness and simplicity. Pille requested having images on the header background, available contact form and modestly placed pricelist.
Website remained on the interactive prototype level in Figma as Voog possibilities were a bit limited.

Tutors: Oliver Maaker, Pille Pesti, Kristjan Jansen.
